Strategy Overview

Some Weather Combo is a "Time-Based Momentum Strategy."
I call it "Some Weather" because is uses only Commodity, Currency and Bond ETF's.
I've puposly left out Equities because they're handeled in several other strategies, within the portfolio.

Logic & Design

Some Weather Combo is comprised of 2 Time-Series Momentum groups using 6 Commodity ETF's, 6 Currency ETF's and 9 Bond ETF's.
The custom momentum factor is the same for both groups, except for the look-backs, fast and slow.
The ETF's are ranked in their group and sector. Each strategy holds rank 1 until it drops to rank 4.
If the position is underwater, a -8% hard stop is in the market.

Key Characteristics

What I like about Some Weather Combo is it's ability to weather the storms of market volatility and come out on top.
Trades can happen at any time and whipsaws are few.
Though it's only been live for 7 month's, the Win Rate is 60%. Average trade length is 41 days.

Performance Notes

I believe that having these sectors in a portfolio is a must.
It's not going to be what drives the account in bull markets. But it will be there, picking up the trends in sector momentum.
