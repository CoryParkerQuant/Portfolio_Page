Slope MR

Systematic Trading Strategy

Strategy Overview

I'm actually not very fond of mean reversion strategies. And that's why I have so many of them.
They all take really small positions but together, they pull their weight.
I also will keep them in the testing phase a bit longer than most other strategies.
But in order to be diversified, I believe it's important to have Mean Reversion in the portfolio.

Logic & Design

Slope MR simply measures the slope of the closes for the past few bars and looks to take a quick profit at the beginning of an up swing.
Slope MR only trades TQQQ.

Key Characteristics

Slope MR is meant to be a quick mean reversion strategy.
The back tested model had a Win Rate of 64% and the average trade length was 2 days.

Performance Notes

Slope MR has only been live for a few months, so live data is lacking at the moment.
Back tested Portfolio correlation is 0.40
