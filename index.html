Trading Philosophy & Approach

When an idea for a strategy comes to me, whether from reading a paper, seeing it in forum, hearing it on a podcast, reading it in a book, seeing it on YouTube, my first though is, "That sounds like a good coding challenge!"
While I'm coding, I get a deeper understanding of what market weakness is being exploited. This then leads me to be aware of the ways it could be over-fit. Which leads me to parameter selection.
If optimization is necessary, I will use RealTest to show me the range of parameter Net Profit, ROR, MaxDD, MAR, Expectancy. Then I will attempt to use the middle of the flattest range.
