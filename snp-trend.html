Strategy Overview

SnP Trend is a Long Only, Moving Average Crossover Strategy.
It's the system I've tried to break many times and look for a reason to kick it out of the portfolio but it just won't die.
It's proof that simple rules equal better results.

Logic & Design

SnP Trend uses 2 moving averages and only trades SCHG.
It is literally 4 lines of code in RealTest. EMA1, EMA2, cross to enter, cross to exit.
In Excel, it was MUCH more but had the same results.

Key Characteristics

If the S&P 500 is in an uptrend, SnP Trend is on it.
Like most trend systems, it will get whipsawed in sideways markets.
For 7 month's, the Win Rate is 66%. Average trade length is 62 days

Performance Notes

Unfortunately, I have not kept live records of SnP Trend this whole time.
And the live data I do have shows where I reduced the size mid year and performance volatility flattens out.
Portfolio correlation is on the higher side at 0.76.
